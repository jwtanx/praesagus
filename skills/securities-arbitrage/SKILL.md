# Securities Arbitrage Skill

## Objective
Identify relative-value opportunities and event-driven trades that exploit
short-term mispricing between equities, ETFs, derivatives, or corporate events.

## Outputs
- Arbitrage / pair-trade thesis
- Catalyst timeline and sizing guidance
- P&L risk analysis
- Execution and financing considerations
- Hedge and termination plan

## Required Sections
1. Trade thesis
2. Price relationship and valuation gap
3. Event or catalyst frame
4. Risk/reward profile
5. Balance sheet and funding analysis
6. Exit criteria

## Research Rules
- Quantify the spread or mispricing clearly.
- Explain the payoff structure and how the trade is expected to converge.
- Identify counterparty, funding, and liquidity risks.
- Include a stop-loss or downside protection plan.

## Output Template
- Trade type: merger arbitrage, convertible arb, dividend capture,
  statistical pair trade, or special situation.
- Entry thesis
- Expected holding period
- Risk controls
- Return potential
