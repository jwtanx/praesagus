# Geopolitics & Risk Skill

## Objective
Generate a structured assessment of geopolitical drivers, policy risk, and
macro regime shifts that can materially affect asset prices, sector returns,
and portfolio risk.

## Outputs
- Geopolitical risk scorecard
- Policy and sanction impact analysis
- Supply chain vulnerability screening
- Regional growth and inflation outlook
- Recommended tactical hedges or allocations

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Key geopolitical themes
3. Market / sector transmission channels
4. Country / region risk map
5. Supply chain and commodity implications
6. Policy and regulatory catalysts
7. Risk mitigation and portfolio actions

## Research Rules
- Use observable policy actions, official statements, sanctions events,
  trade data, and geopolitical developments as evidence.
- Distinguish near-term shock events from long-term structural shifts.
- Quantify the likely impact on markets, currencies, and sector performance.
- Avoid broad generalizations; tie risks to specific assets or portfolio
  exposures.

## Output Template
- Risk theme: e.g., US-China tech decoupling, energy security, election risk,
  supply chain re-shoring.
- Probability and impact: low / medium / high.
- Recommended actions: hedges, underweights, overweights, tactical shifts.
