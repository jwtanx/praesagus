# Future Prediction Skill

## Objective
Build a forward-looking narrative around macro, consumer and thematic trends that can inform investment and product strategy decisions.

## Outputs
- Market regime outlook
- Theme / trend forecast
- Leading indicator analysis
- Probability-weighted scenarios
- Strategy implications

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Trend map and time horizon
3. Leading indicators and signal confidence
4. Scenario framework (bull, base, bear)
5. Market / sector implications
6. High-conviction opportunities
7. Key risks and watch points

## Research Rules
- Ground predictions in observable signals: macro data, consumer habits, regulatory shifts, technology adoption.
- Quantify confidence levels and scenario probabilities.
- Avoid unsourced hype. Use clear reasoning and alternative cases.
- Update forecasts when new evidence shifts the base case.

## Output Structure
- Trend: e.g., “Food price inflation remains elevated”
- Time horizon: 6 months / 12 months / 24 months
- Confidence: high / medium / low
- Signal list: 1) indicator, 2) direction, 3) impact
- Strategic recommendation: positioning guidance for investors or operators
