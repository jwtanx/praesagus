# Commodity Insights Skill

## Objective
Produce a comprehensive commodity market intelligence report that identifies
price drivers, inventory dynamics, macro correlations, and strategic
investment implications for commodity-related equities, ETFs, and physical
supply chains.

## Outputs
- Commodity price outlook and cycle signal
- Fundamental supply/demand balance analysis
- Inventory and production trend assessment
- Macro correlation and inflation signal review
- Buy/hold/sell thesis for related equities or funds

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Price action and market structure
3. Supply-side dynamics
4. Demand-side dynamics
5. Macro and currency linkage
6. Inventory or stockpile trends
7. ESG / regulatory impact
8. Investment implications and recommended instruments

## Research Rules
- Prefer official data sources: EIA, IEA, USDA, CME, commodity exchanges,
  government inventory reports, and publicly disclosed producer data.
- Compare current supply/demand with five-year seasonal averages.
- Use hard ratios such as stocks-to-use, days of supply, import dependence,
  and production capacity utilization.
- Translate commodity views into practical investment ideas: miners,
  refiners, logistics, consumer price exposure, or hedged funds.
- Flag market-driven risk events (weather, sanctions, strikes) separately.

## Output Template
- Commodity: e.g., Brent crude, copper, corn, lithium.
- Trend: bullish / neutral / bearish.
- Key drivers: supply, demand, policy, inventory, FX, macro.
- Scenario outlook: base / bullish / bearish.
- Recommended vehicle: equity, ETF, futures spread, or avoid.
