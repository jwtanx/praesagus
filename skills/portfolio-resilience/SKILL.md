# Portfolio Resilience Skill

## Objective
Generate defensive portfolio guidance, cash flow resilience analysis, and
scenario-based asset mix recommendations for uncertain macro environments.

## Outputs
- Resilience scorecard for sectors or names
- Inflation and recession vulnerability analysis
- Defensive asset selection guidance
- Rebalancing / risk management rules
- Scenario-based portfolio recommendations

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Macro risk assessment
3. Resilience criteria and metrics
4. Defensive exposures and quality names
5. Cash flow and balance sheet strength
6. Risk management and rebalancing guidance
7. Portfolio construction implication

## Research Rules
- Emphasize companies with strong free cash flow, low leverage, and pricing
  power.
- Distinguish between pure defensive utility names, quality cyclical stocks, and
  inflation-hedged exposures.
- Provide a credible stress scenario and how the proposed portfolio behaves.
- Avoid generic ‘‘defensive’’ recommendations without clear metrics.

## Output Template
- Resilience rating: high / medium / low.
- Core holdings and satellite ideas.
- Target risk allocation: conservative / balanced / opportunistic.
- Rebalance triggers: valuation, macro signal, or tactical drawdown.
