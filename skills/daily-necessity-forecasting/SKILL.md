# Daily Necessity Forecasting Skill

## Objective
Analyze consumption, retailer inventory, and pricing signals to forecast demand for everyday essentials and daily necessity categories.

## Outputs
- Demand trend summary for key categories
- Supply chain risk assessment
- Consumer sentiment drivers
- Resilient brand/product call-outs
- Short-term and medium-term demand outlook

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Category spotlight (e.g., groceries, household supplies, fuel, utilities)
3. Demand drivers and leading indicators
4. Retail and pricing observations
5. Supply chain stress points
6. Consistency and resilience rating
7. Forecast horizon: 1 week, 1 month, 3 months

## Research Rules
- Prioritize objective data: retail sales, CPI, commodity costs, inventory cycles.
- Use surveys or anecdotal evidence sparingly and label them clearly.
- Do not overstate certainty; present scenarios when signals are mixed.
- Quantify downside vs upside with scenarios and probability ranges.

## Output Format
- Category: e.g., groceries, cleaning products, personal care, energy
- Trend: rising / stable / falling
- Signal strength: strong / moderate / weak
- Primary drivers
- Recommendation: defensive stock picks, supply plays, or cash preservation
