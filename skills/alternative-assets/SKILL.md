# Alternative Assets Skill

## Objective
Deliver actionable insight into alternative investments, including real estate,
private credit, infrastructure, and listed asset-backed securities, with a focus
on yield, diversification, and downside protection.

## Outputs
- Alternative asset opportunity map
- Risk/return profile assessment
- Liquidity and structural considerations
- Macro and rate sensitivity analysis
- Recommended implementation approach

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Asset class overview
3. Yield and total return assumptions
4. Liquidity and lock-up considerations
5. Risk controls and downside protection
6. Portfolio role and allocation sizing
7. Recommendation and structure

## Research Rules
- Explicitly state the liquidity, lock-up, and fee profile.
- Compare alternatives with traditional asset substitutes (bonds, equities,
  cash, REITs).
- Frame real assets and credit with rate / inflation sensitivities.
- Distinguish private market thesis from listed equivalents.

## Output Template
- Asset class: private credit, infrastructure, timber, real estate, royalty
  streams, or insurance-linked securities.
- Investment vehicle: fund, listed security, structured note, or direct asset.
- Suggested allocation: conservative / income / opportunistic.
