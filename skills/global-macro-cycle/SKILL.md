# Global Macro Cycle Skill

## Objective
Deliver an integrated macro cycle view that connects global growth, inflation,
monetary and fiscal policy, and structural geopolitical drivers to asset-class
and regional investment guidance.

## Outputs
- Global macro cycle diagnosis
- Regional growth and inflation mapping
- Policy regime and rate trajectory outlook
- Commodity and FX flow implications
- Multi-asset allocation guidance

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Cycle stage and macro drivers
3. Regional divergence analysis
4. Policy and central bank outlook
5. Commodity / FX / credit transmission
6. Portfolio impact and allocation tilts
7. Risk scenarios and thematic watch list

## Research Rules
- Use global economic data, central bank commentary, fiscal stimulus signals, and
  trade flows.
- Distinguish between cyclical drivers and structural drivers.
- Evaluate how different regions and asset classes are positioned for the cycle.
- Include commodity and currency implications for cross-border investors.
- Present a clear multi-asset or regional allocation implication.

## Output Template
- Cycle phase: early expansion / mid-cycle / late-cycle / contraction.
- Regional stance: overweight / underweight / neutral.
- Asset implication: equities / bonds / commodities / FX / alternatives.
- Key risks: policy shock, inflation surprise, growth gap.
