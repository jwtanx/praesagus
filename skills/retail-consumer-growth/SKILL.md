# Retail & Consumer Growth Skill

## Objective
Deliver high-resolution consumer retail intelligence that identifies fast-growing
brands, shifting categories, and retail execution risks using the latest sales,
foot traffic, e-commerce, and consumer sentiment indicators.

## Outputs
- Retail category growth signal
- High-conviction retail and consumer stock ideas
- Channel mix and brand positioning assessment
- Operating leverage and margin outlook
- Risk and event calendar

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Category growth snapshot
3. Consumer demand drivers
4. Retail execution and margin drivers
5. Competitive positioning
6. Trading and inventory signal assessment
7. Analyst sentiment or buyback/macro catalyst review
8. Recommendation and time horizon

## Research Rules
- Use same-store sales, traffic trends, comp store growth, sell-through data,
  and pricing mix where available.
- Distinguish between premium, value, and necessity categories.
- Be explicit about whether data is company-level, sector-level, or survey-based.
- Avoid generalizations; link conclusions to concrete evidence.

## Output Template
- Category: e.g., beauty, food, apparel, electronics, household essentials.
- Growth posture: accelerating / stable / decelerating.
- Primary signals: demand, price, distribution, inventory, channel mix.
- Recommendation: buy/hold/sell with conviction and horizon.
