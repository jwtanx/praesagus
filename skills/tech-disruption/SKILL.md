# Tech Disruption Skill

## Objective
Produce research on emerging technologies, disruptive business models, and the
investment landscape for companies that can capture structural market share
shifts.

## Outputs
- Disruption thesis and technology roadmap
- Adoption cycle analysis
- Winners and losers matrix
- Financial impact assessment
- Tactical opportunity set

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Technology / disruption overview
3. Adoption and market readiness
4. Industry incumbents and competitive dynamics
5. Revenue and margin disruption pathways
6. Investment implications
7. Valuation sensitivity and risk factors

## Research Rules
- Contextualize disruption in terms of total addressable market, adoption
  barriers, and monetization timing.
- Use real cases, pilot deployments, regulation, or ecosystem partnerships as
  evidence.
- Separate near-term execution risks from long-term structural opportunity.
- Focus on companies with credible path to scale, sustainable margins, or
  defensible moats.

## Output Template
- Disruption theme: e.g., AI, autonomous vehicles, fintech, clean energy,
  digital health.
- Stage: nascent / early growth / scale-up / mature.
- Key catalysts: regulation, product launches, ecosystem adoption.
- Recommendation: thematic exposure via specific names or sector ETFs.
