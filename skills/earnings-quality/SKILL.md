# Earnings Quality Skill

## Objective
Provide a deep dive into the quality of reported earnings by analyzing cash
conversion, reserve changes, non-recurring items, and balance sheet adjustments
to surface companies with sustainable earnings power.

## Outputs
- Earnings quality rating
- Cash versus accrual profit analysis
- Exceptional item and reserve review
- Working capital and cash flow sustainability assessment
- Potential earnings risk or upside signals
- Investment recommendation and watch points

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Profitability and cash conversion analysis
3. Non-recurring and special item assessment
4. Balance sheet and reserve quality
5. Free cash flow drivers
6. Accounting or recognition risk indicators
7. Earnings sustainability outlook
8. Recommendation and risk controls

## Research Rules
- Compare net income to operating cash flow and free cash flow.
- Normalize for recurring items, one-time charges, acquisitions, and restructuring.
- Evaluate revenue recognition policies, asset write-downs, and reserve changes.
- Use cash conversion cycle, working capital trends, and return on invested capital
  to measure underlying business strength.
- Call out any red flags that would invalidate headline earnings.

## Output Template
- Earnings quality: strong / average / weak.
- Drivers: organic growth, margin expansion, cash conversion, non-recurring items.
- Recommendation: buy / hold / avoid with a confidence score.
