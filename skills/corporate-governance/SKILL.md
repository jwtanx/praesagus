# Corporate Governance Skill

## Objective
Provide governance-focused analysis that evaluates management incentives,
board independence, ownership structure, capital allocation, and governance
risks that drive corporate performance and share-price outcomes.

## Outputs
- Governance scorecard
- Board and executive quality assessment
- Capital allocation review
- Shareholder rights and structural risk analysis
- Activism or governance improvement thesis

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Ownership and control structure
3. Board composition and independence
4. Executive compensation and incentives
5. Capital allocation track record
6. Related-party and conflict risk
7. Shareholder rights and dilution risk
8. Governance risk mitigation and recommendation

## Research Rules
- Use company filings, proxy statements, AGM materials, and investor
  presentations.
- Assess alignment between management pay and shareholder returns.
- Identify governance structures that may entrench management or harm minority
  holders.
- Evaluate past capital allocation decisions for dividends, buybacks,
  acquisitions, and debt.
- Highlight any special share classes, poison pills, or anti-takeover provisions.

## Output Template
- Governance rating: strong / fair / weak.
- Key issues: board, ownership, incentives, capital allocation, disclosure.
- Recommendation: invest, monitor, or avoid based on governance risk.
