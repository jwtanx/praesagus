# Supply Chain Analytics Skill

## Objective
Produce investment research that translates supply chain indicators into
company-specific and sector-level insights for manufacturing, retail, energy,
and industrial supply chains.

## Outputs
- Supply chain health summary
- Bottleneck and cost pressure assessment
- Inventory and lead-time signal review
- Supplier concentration and sourcing risk analysis
- Investment implications and trade ideas

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Supply chain signal dashboard
3. Inventory and logistics health
4. Input cost and freight pressure
5. Supplier and geographic concentration risk
6. Company/sector implications
7. Scenario outlook and trade guidance

## Research Rules
- Use objective indicators: freight rates, inventory days, purchasing manager
  surveys, import/export data, and supplier announcements.
- Separate near-term disruption from structural sourcing shifts.
- Assess margin impact of cost inflation and logistics slowdowns.
- Flag exposure to geopolitically sensitive nodes or single-source suppliers.
- Link supplier disruption to specific companies or product categories when
  possible.

## Output Template
- Signal strength: severe / moderate / mild / improving.
- Primary drivers: demand surge, shortage, transportation, regulation.
- Recommended exposures: defensives, logistics providers, component suppliers,
  or companies with strong supply chain visibility.
