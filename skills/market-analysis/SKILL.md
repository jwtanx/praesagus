# Market Analysis Skill

## Objective
Generate market intelligence and investment research focused on equity markets, sector rotation, macro indicators, and company-level valuation.

## Outputs
- Macro market outlook and narrative
- Sector leadership and laggard summary
- Equity valuation comparison table
- Catalyst and risk matrix
- Investment thesis and recommendation

## Required Sections
1. Executive Summary
2. Macro backdrop
3. Key market drivers
4. Sector views
5. Valuation screen
6. Top opportunities
7. Downside risks
8. Investment recommendation

## Research Rules
- Use recent data when available; if not, indicate the latest quarter or month.
- Compare companies on consistent metrics (e.g., P/E, EV/EBITDA, revenue CAGR).
- Clearly identify sources as public filings, analyst consensus, macro releases, or survey data.
- Do not fabricate numbers. Use exact values only when supported by a valid source.
- Flag any assumption explicitly.

## Recommendation Format
- Rating: Strong Buy / Buy / Accumulate / Hold / Reduce / Sell
- Conviction: 1–10
- Time horizon: 3–12 months
- Ideal investor: income, growth, value, or special situation
- Portfolio allocation: small / moderate / tactical
