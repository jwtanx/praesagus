# Inflation Opportunity Skill

## Objective
Build a pricing-power and inflation-resilience research note that identifies
sectors, companies, and assets likely to outperform in inflationary or
stagflationary regimes.

## Outputs
- Inflation regime assessment
- Pricing power and margin durability analysis
- Asset class and sector opportunity map
- Real return thesis for equities, commodities, and real assets
- Risk scenarios and hedging guidance

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Inflation drivers and momentum
3. Pricing power and cost pass-through analysis
4. Best-in-class inflation-resilient exposures
5. Vulnerable sectors and companies
6. Real asset and yield alternative review
7. Policy and rate sensitivity
8. Recommended portfolio positioning

## Research Rules
- Distinguish between demand-pull and supply-push inflation sources.
- Evaluate corporate pricing power using gross margin expansion, contract
  indexing, and market share protection.
- Prioritize cash-flow resilient businesses and real assets with inflation-linked
  returns.
- Use real-term return projections and compare to nominal returns.
- Flag timing risk if inflation is expected to be transitory.

## Output Template
- Inflation signal: rising / peaking / stabilizing / falling.
- Opportunity set: consumer staples, energy, materials, financials, real assets.
- Recommended exposures: equities, REITs, commodities, TIPS-like instruments.
- Strategic guidance: overweight / underweight / hedge with conviction.
