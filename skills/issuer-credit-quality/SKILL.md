# Issuer Credit Quality Skill

## Objective
Provide a detailed credit analysis framework that evaluates issuer solvency,
liquidity, refinancing risk, covenant protection, and compensation for credit
risk across public and private debt issuers.

## Outputs
- Credit quality rating and relative value signal
- Leverage and coverage analysis
- Liquidity and refinancing risk profile
- Covenant and structural protection review
- Spread / yield opportunity assessment
- Recommendation and position sizing guidance

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Issuer profile and capital structure
3. Leverage and coverage metrics
4. Cash flow generation and liquidity buffers
5. Covenant and event risk analysis
6. Spread vs rating and peer group
7. Refinancing and maturity wall risk
8. Valuation or implied default view
9. Recommendation and risk-adjusted yield thesis

## Research Rules
- Use issuer financial statements, bond indentures, ratings agency commentary,
  and recent refinancing activity.
- Quantify leverage using EBITDA-based metrics, net debt, and fixed-charge
  coverage ratios.
- Evaluate liquidity separately: cash, undrawn facilities, free cash flow, and
  covenant headroom.
- For structured or hybrid securities, analyze payment priority, call features,
  and equity conversion optionality.
- Be explicit when credit views depend on macro rates, collateral values, or
  cyclical revenue.

## Output Template
- Issuer: name, sector, rating.
- Security: bond, note, preferred, tranche.
- Yield / spread: current levels and historical context.
- Credit rating: strong / average / weak with rationale.
- Recommendation: buy / hold / avoid and suggested weight.
