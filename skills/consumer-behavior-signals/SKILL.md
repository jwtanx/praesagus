# Consumer Behavior Signals Skill

## Objective
Translate consumer demand signals into investment insights for retailers,
consumer goods, e-commerce platforms, and service providers.

## Outputs
- Demand signal summary
- Category and brand winners
- Revenue and margin read-throughs
- Consumer sentiment and pricing pressure analysis
- Investment implications and timing

## Required Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Consumer demand signal dashboard
3. Category momentum and weakness
4. Pricing / inflation pass-through analysis
5. Winner / loser assessment
6. Short-term and medium-term outlook
7. Investment recommendation

## Research Rules
- Prioritize actual transaction data, search frequency, foot traffic, app usage,
  and retail inventory signals.
- Separate cyclical consumer confidence signals from secular value shifts.
- Be transparent when using proxy data or anecdotal evidence.
- Emphasize evidence-backed ideas rather than generic consumer optimism.

## Output Template
- Signal: e.g., spending, traffic, search, pricing, sentiment.
- Strength: strong / moderate / weak.
- Recommendation: buy / hold / avoid with conviction and horizon.
