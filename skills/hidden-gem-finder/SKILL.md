# Hidden Gem Finder Skill

## Objective
Uncover overlooked stocks with strong fundamentals, attractive relative valuation,
and catalysts that could generate outperformance versus benchmark indices.

## Outputs
- Hidden gem candidate shortlist
- Fundamental quality screen
- Catalyst / re-rating thesis
- Valuation gap analysis
- Risk-adjusted recommendation

## Required Sections
1. Summary of thesis
2. Why the market is overlooking this name
3. Financial quality and growth profile
4. Valuation comparison vs peers
5. Catalysts and trigger events
6. Execution risks
7. Target price and time horizon
8. Suitability profile

## Screening Criteria
- Low analyst coverage or low liquidity relative to peers
- Improving margins, earnings revisions, or cash flow acceleration
- Strong balance sheet or deleveraging path
- Reasonable valuation relative to growth and asset quality
- Clear catalyst (product launch, strategic deal, restructuring, buyback)

## Recommendation Format
- Rating: Strong Buy / Buy / Accumulate / Hold
- Conviction: 1–10
- Target horizon: 6–18 months
- Suggested allocation: opportunistic / tactical
